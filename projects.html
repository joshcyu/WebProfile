Web-Based Passenger Tracker and Monitoring RFID-Enabled Card System

The project's goal is to provide a system that will help the Islanders boarding process by preventing boat overloading, cashless fare transaction, and reduction of long line by manually logging or filling the log book at the port entrance. The project was made to serve as a preventive measure and avoid the past incident that happened at Binangonan Rizal, it is dedicated to enhance passenger safety by tracking their boarding and arrival time and date at each port.
